An interactive **Tableau dashboard** built to analyze *Zepto's product inventory, pricing, discounts, and category-wise product availability*. This dashboard helps understand inventory distribution, pricing trends, and product assortment across different categories.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Zepto's product catalog to answer key business questions such as:

* Which categories have the highest number of products?
* Which product categories have the highest Average MRP?
* How is inventory distributed across categories?
* Which products have the highest available quantity?
* What is the overall inventory size and discount percentage?

The dashboard provides an easy-to-understand visual summary for inventory management and pricing analysis.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Products by Category

Displays the number of products available in each category.

**Insights**

* Munchies has the largest product assortment.
* Cooking Essentials also contains a large number of products.
* Personal Care and Paan Corner have comparatively fewer products.

---

### 2. Average MRP by Category

Shows the average selling price (MRP) across categories.

**Insights**

* Premium categories have the highest average MRP.
* Budget categories show relatively lower pricing.

---

### 3. Inventory by Category

Visualizes available inventory across all categories.

Top inventory categories include:

* Cooking Essentials
* Munchies
* Chocolates & Candies
* Ice Cream & Desserts
* Packaged Food

Lowest inventory:

* Meats, Fish & Eggs

---

### 4. Top 10 Products

Highlights products with the highest available quantity.

Useful for identifying:

* Best stocked products
* High inventory items
* Potential fast-moving products

---

### 5. Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes filters for:

* Category
* Quantity Range
* Average MRP
* Product Count

These filters allow users to explore specific product segments dynamically.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* Zepto offers products across **1,669 unique items**.
* Total available inventory exceeds **14,000 units**.
* **Cooking Essentials** and **Munchies** dominate inventory.
* Several categories maintain significantly higher average pricing than others.
* **453 products are currently out of stock**, indicating opportunities for inventory replenishment.
* Interactive filtering enables detailed category-level analysis.
